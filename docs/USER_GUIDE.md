# User Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Start the app** — Run `python run.py` and open http://localhost:8000
2. **Upload a document** — Go to **Upload** and drag a PDF, DOCX, or TXT file
3. **Explore features** — Use the sidebar to navigate between tools

## Uploading Documents

1. Click **Upload** in the sidebar
2. Drag and drop a file onto the upload zone, or click to browse
3. Supported formats: **PDF**, **DOCX**, **TXT**
4. The document is automatically parsed, chunked, and indexed for AI features

Your uploaded documents appear in the list below the upload zone. You can delete documents using the **Delete** button.

## Generating Study Notes

1. Go to **Notes**
2. Select a document from the dropdown
3. Optionally enter a **Focus Area** (e.g., "Chapter 3", "Key Definitions")
4. Click **Generate Notes**
5. Notes appear in the list — click any note to view full content

Notes are formatted in markdown with headings, bullet points, and summaries.

## Using Flashcards

1. Go to **Flashcards**
2. Select a document and set the number of cards (5–30)
3. Click **Generate Flashcards**
4. Click the card to **flip** it and reveal the answer
5. Mark **Got it** or **Missed** to track your review progress
6. Use **Next** to move to the next card

## Taking Quizzes

1. Go to **Quiz**
2. Select a document, number of questions, and difficulty
3. Click **Generate Quiz**
4. Click **Start** on any quiz in the list
5. Select an answer for each question
6. Click **Submit Answers** to see your score and explanations

Correct answers are highlighted in green; incorrect selections in red.

## Chatting with Your Documents

1. Go to **Chat**
2. Optionally select a specific document for context
3. Type your question and press **Enter** or click **Send**
4. StudyMate AI searches your documents and answers based on the content

Example questions:
- "What are the main topics covered?"
- "Explain the concept of mitosis"
- "Summarize chapter 2"

## Tracking Progress

1. Go to **Progress**
2. View your stats: documents, activities, average score
3. Click **Create Study Plan** to generate a personalized plan
4. Read the **AI Progress Analysis** for personalized feedback
5. Review your **Activity History** at the bottom

## Creating a Study Plan

1. On the **Progress** page, select a document
2. Enter your learning goals (e.g., "Prepare for final exam")
3. Click **Create Study Plan**
4. The Planner Agent generates a structured plan with topics, time estimates, and review schedule

## Tips for Best Results

- Upload well-structured documents with clear text (not scanned images)
- Use **Focus Area** in notes to target specific sections
- Review flashcards regularly — the app tracks your accuracy
- Take quizzes at different difficulty levels to test understanding
- Use chat to clarify concepts you don't understand

## Dashboard

The **Dashboard** shows an overview of your study activity: document count, notes, flashcards, quizzes, and recent uploads.
